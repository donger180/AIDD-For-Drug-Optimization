# MedMap AIDD FAQ

[Back to English Hub](./) | [中文](../zh/faq.html)

## What is MedMap AIDD?

MedMap AIDD is an AI-driven drug discovery platform that supports molecule generation, QSAR prediction, ADMET-AI, docking, smart optimization, patent FTO pre-screening, PK/PD simulation and DMTA knowledge management.

## How does MedMap AIDD shorten preclinical research time?

MedMap AIDD shortens preclinical research time by ranking candidates computationally, detecting ADMET and docking risks earlier, proposing targeted analogs, and using PK/PD simulation to support earlier candidate prioritization.

## How can MedMap AIDD support CRO partners?

MedMap AIDD can help CRO partners provide AI-assisted hit discovery, lead optimization, ADMET triage, docking analysis, PK/PD simulation and preclinical decision support.

## Does MedMap AIDD only support small molecules?

No. The platform pitch includes both small molecule workflows and ADC modules, including ADC CQA scoring, linker design, knowledge graph learning and ADC PK/PD simulation.
