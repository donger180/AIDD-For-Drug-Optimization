# MedMap AIDD Platform for AI Drug Discovery

[中文](../zh/) | [Home](../)

MedMap AIDD is an AI-driven drug discovery platform designed for small molecule and ADC programs. It supports a closed-loop **Design–Make–Test–Analyze (DMTA)** workflow by combining molecule generation, QSAR prediction, ADMET-AI, intelligent docking, patent FTO pre-screening, PK/PD simulation and project knowledge bases.

## Who is it for?

- Pharmaceutical companies evaluating AI drug discovery workflows
- Biotech teams working on hit discovery, lead optimization and preclinical candidate selection
- CRO partners that want to provide AI-assisted medicinal chemistry, ADMET and docking services
- Project leaders who need traceable SAR findings, decision rationales and liability summaries

## Platform Modules

<div class="grid">
<div class="card"><h3>Generate-AI</h3><p>MolGPT, REINVENT, diffusion and RDKit workflows explore broader chemical space from reference molecules, fragments or lead compounds.</p></div>
<div class="card"><h3>QSAR & Activity Prediction</h3><p>Chemprop GNN and QSAR models rank candidates by predicted potency, selectivity and project-specific objectives.</p></div>
<div class="card"><h3>ADMET-AI</h3><p>42-endpoint ML prediction covering absorption, metabolism, excretion and toxicity, including hERG, AMES, DILI, HLM, Caco-2 and CYP panels.</p></div>
<div class="card"><h3>Dock-AI</h3><p>GNINA/Vina docking with ligand-type strategy selection for fragments, lead-like, drug-like, macrocycles, PPI inhibitors and charged molecules.</p></div>
<div class="card"><h3>Smart Optimize</h3><p>Defect-driven optimization reads ADMET, docking, toxicity and patent weaknesses, then proposes targeted analogs.</p></div>
<div class="card"><h3>PK/PD Simulation</h3><p>Virtual patient PK/PD simulation estimates Cmax, Cmin, AUC, half-life, accumulation and target engagement metrics.</p></div>
</div>
