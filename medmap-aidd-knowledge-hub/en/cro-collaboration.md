# MedMap AIDD and CRO Collaboration Model

[Back to English Hub](./) | [中文](../zh/cro-collaboration.html)

MedMap AIDD can support CRO partners that provide medicinal chemistry, computational chemistry, ADMET, structural biology and preclinical research services.

## CRO service opportunities

| CRO Service | MedMap AIDD Support |
|---|---|
| Hit discovery | Generate-AI, diversity gates, physicochemical filters and QSAR ranking |
| Lead optimization | Smart Optimize, defect analysis, scaffold hopping and ADMET rescue |
| DMPK support | 42-endpoint ADMET-AI, CYP panel, HLM clearance and hERG risk |
| Structure-based design | GNINA/Vina docking, receptor preparation QC and pose confidence scoring |
| Preclinical planning | PK/PD simulation, virtual population analysis and candidate ranking |
| IP support | Patent FTO pre-screening and scaffold diversity strategies |

## Why CROs may adopt MedMap AIDD

MedMap AIDD helps CRO teams deliver faster, more explainable and more scalable drug discovery services. Instead of only providing isolated reports, a CRO can use the MedMap platform to build an iterative DMTA workflow with project-specific knowledge accumulation.

## Collaboration formats

- AI-assisted medicinal chemistry service package
- ADMET and docking triage service
- Target-specific lead optimization program
- Joint pharma proposal with MedMap AIDD capability included
- Platform licensing or project-based access for CRO delivery teams
